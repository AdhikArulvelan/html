Fun Things To Build With Snow



Snow Man

Get 5 rocks and keep them for later, along with 2 sticks. Get one big, one medium, and one small snow ball. stack the snowballs from biggest to smallest, finally add 2 rocks for eyes on the smallest snowball, and 3 rocks for buttons on the medium snowball. Add the sticks as arms. For video instruction, click the link Video Demo



Giant Snowball

Get a small snowball, about the size of a small snowball used to build the snow man, and roll it around for about 15 to 20 minutes, in the snow. And there is your giant snowball!



Snow Sculpture

Get tons, and I mean a lot of snow, form it into an about 4 feet renctangle, top to bottom. Then sculpt it using a scalple or trowel. Make it into whatever you want. Here is a video of some people building some snow sculptures Link
